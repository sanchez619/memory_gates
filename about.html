The true nature of Memory

A red gate stands in between the mainland and an island, in the water. Most people have seen and know this picture, but they do not know the name. The image – a seemingly random constellation of items is more memorable than the name of the gate. This is how the memory actually works. As US Memory Champ Joshua Foer says: „Memory is non-linear.“ The brain associates items via various factors, and making sense of the factors is called Mnemonics – The art of remembering. Various memory athletes have shown tremendous feats of memorisation, and we use these techniques to help you not to forget.
